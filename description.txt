Ammo Switch v1.0

Do you ever thought "Argh, it'd be nice to quickly switch ammo!"? No? ok...
But if you do, then this is your choice! Ammo Switch gives you ability to manualy choose, what ammo slot use for shooting. Make your keybinds and switch ammo as you wish!

So, what this mod can do?
Main feature: Allows you to switch ammo type on fly, without opening inventory.
Additional features:
- UI - shows what ammo types in your ammo slots, their total amount and which is currently active.
- Consumption priority - now take ammo of choosen type from basic inventory, then from ammo slots.
- If selected ammo type can't be used with weapon, then usual rules for ammo type apply.
- Multiplayer compitable
- Other mod should be compitable
